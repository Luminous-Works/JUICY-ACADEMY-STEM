# JUICY ACADEMY: ADVANCED COMPOSITES CURRICULUM
## College-Level Materials Science & Engineering

### 1. Materials Selection
*   **AHP Method**: Analytical Hierarchy Process for material selection.
*   **Performance Indices**: Ashby Plots and strength-to-weight optimization.
*   **Case Studies**: Aerospace vs. Automotive structural choices.

### 2. Properties Analysis
*   **Mechanical**: Tensile testing, fracture mechanics, and fatigue limits.
*   **Electrical**: Conductivity in metals vs. semiconductors.
*   **Thermal**: Coefficients of expansion in heterogeneous mixtures.
*   **Magnetic**: Permeability and coercivity in soft vs. hard ferromagnets.

### 3. Smart Materials
*   **Shape Memory Alloys (SMAs)**: Nitinol and its martensitic transformation.
*   **Piezoelectrics**: Energy harvesting and sensor integration.
*   **Chromic Materials**: Photochromic glass and thermochromic coatings.

### 4. Materials Characterization (Lab Protocols)
*   **SEM (Scanning Electron Microscopy)**: Imaging surface morphology at nanometer scales.
*   **XRD (X-Ray Diffraction)**: Determining crystal lattice spacing (Bragg's Law).
*   **EDX (Energy-Dispersive X-ray Spectroscopy)**: Elemental mapping of alloys.
