# JUICY Academy — Artificial Intelligence

**Module AIS · Grade 9–University · Luminous Works LLC**

> *"AI is the story of today, tomorrow, and the next ten thousand years."*

This is the dedicated repository for the JUICY Academy Artificial Intelligence curriculum track.

AI is not a technology subject. It is the subject of our epoch — the convergence point of mathematics, physics, biology, philosophy, ethics, politics, and every question about consciousness, power, and what it means to be human that civilisation has ever asked. This curriculum treats it that way.

Every lesson is grounded in real mathematics. The history is told honestly — including who built these systems, who was erased from the record, and who bears the consequences. The Caribbean and African diaspora are at the centre, not the margin. The ethics are named directly, not softened.

## The Thread

From the Dogon watching Sirius B with naked eyes, to Turing defining computation before computers existed, to six unnamed women programming ENIAC, to the language model reading this sentence — the signal has always been there. The question has always been who was allowed to send it, who was allowed to receive it, and who was credited for the transmission.

This curriculum exists so that the next generation of Jamaican and Caribbean students are not just receiving the signal.

They are the ones who send it.
