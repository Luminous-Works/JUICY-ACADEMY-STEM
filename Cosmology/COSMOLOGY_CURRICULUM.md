### The Premise

Every Luminous.Works synthesizer is a physics instrument before it is a musical one.

PLASMA runs on fractional differential equations. SINGULARITY simulates gravitational collapse. PARALLAX uses real Keplerian orbital mechanics. The REDSHIFT surface calibrates to the rotational frequency of an actual neutron star 280 light-years from Earth.

This is not metaphor. This is not branding. These are the equations.

The goal of this module is to teach the physics before the student encounters it in the instrument — so that when they turn a knob and hear the sound change, they understand *why* at the level of mathematics.

A student who completes this module will understand:
- How a dead star becomes the most precise clock in the universe
- Why every galaxy is moving away from us and what that means for sound
- How matter at near-absolute-zero stops being many things and becomes one
- Why the calculus invented in 1695 describes oscillations that classical ADSR cannot
- What happens to matter at the boundary of a black hole, and why SINGULARITY uses this

### Why This Belongs in a Music School

Sound is pressure oscillation through a medium. Light is electromagnetic oscillation through spacetime. Both are waves. Both obey the same underlying mathematics.

The Doppler effect that shifts a police siren as it passes you is the same equation that astronomers use to measure galactic recession. The resonance that collapsed Broughton Bridge (SP-01) is the same resonance that makes neutron stars spin down over millions of years. The phase coherence of a Bose-Einstein Condensate is the same concept as unison oscillator stacking in a synthesizer.

Physics is not a separate subject from music. It is the deeper layer of the same subject.
