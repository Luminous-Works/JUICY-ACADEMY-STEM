# JUICY Academy — Women in STEM

**Module WIS · Grade 9–University · Luminous Works LLC**

> *"These women advanced human civilisation. Most curricula do not teach their names. That ends here."*

This is the dedicated repository for the JUICY Academy Women in STEM curriculum track.

Every lesson in this module is rigorous science — real physics, real mathematics, real discoveries. The history is not a supplement to the science. It is inseparable from it. Understanding why Jocelyn Bell's name was absent from the Nobel announcement requires understanding exactly what she discovered and how. Understanding why GPS works requires understanding the mathematics Gladys West built. Understanding why Dr. Mae Jemison was exceptional requires understanding what chemical engineers and physicians actually do.

## Caribbean and African Diaspora Connections

- **Dr. Patricia Bath** — father Rupert Bath, Trinidadian; first Black female medical patent holder in the United States
- **Dr. Mae Jemison** — African American heritage rooted in the Black American South; Peace Corps physician in West Africa
- **Katherine Johnson, Dorothy Vaughan, Mary Jackson** — African American women who built the mathematical backbone of the US space programme
