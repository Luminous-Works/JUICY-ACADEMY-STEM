Protecting Jamaica's Natural Heritage in Real Time

JUICY provides real-time environmental monitoring, scientific data collection, and conservation infrastructure for Jamaica's four critical natural sites. Our mission: secure UNESCO World Heritage status for Cockpit Country, Glistening Waters, Blue Mountains, and Green Grotto.

UNESCO World Heritage Pathway

JUICY provides the scientific documentation and monitoring infrastructure required for UNESCO World Heritage designation. Our data demonstrates ecological significance, uniqueness, and protection mechanisms.

By establishing real-time monitoring and comprehensive environmental documentation, Jamaica strengthens its case for protecting these irreplaceable ecosystems at the highest international level.

The Goal: Transform Jamaica from visitor of international conservation frameworks into steward of globally recognized natural heritage.
